Comments

Comments are used for human understanding purpose. They are not exectuted i.e. they do not disturbes the program's execution. Writing comments is a good habit, It helps you to sometimes even understand your own program :-) .

Single line comment :

int a;
  • // This is a comment!
    • a = 1; // This is also a comment!

    Multiline comments:

  • /* This is multiline comment. It has
    nothing to do with the program. */
    •
