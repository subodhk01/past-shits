PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE
It is my personal experience that reading habit is the soul of learning. Books are silent teachers that provide butter of information skimmed from rich experience & yawning thoughts. New generation is equipped with more comfortable learning tools viz. different forms of audio visual systems, internet & television being the most popular among them, where least efforts are required to assimilate readymade knowledge. In our school students are encouraged for self-learning through child centric methodologies and are encouraged to cooperate and coordinate with their peers to compete in a healthy environment.
MISSION
MGPS is established with a mission of providing world class environment and learning opportunities to children with Sanskaras based on Indian culture, patriotism, moral values and world brotherhood at a reasonable fees. The school endeavors to provide excellent schooling through a team of passionate teachers and the state of art infrastructural facilities. The school offers various focused activities to develop the scientific aptitude, provides guidance on having a healthy life style and have established a set up to provide seasonal fruit juices, sprouts, sweet corns, soup for promoting healthy food habits. Recognizing the need of the continuous professional development of the teachers, the school executes consistent training plans for updating the teaching staff and empowering them to deal with the student queries and to do justice.
ABOUT US
With the blessings and inspiration of the sublime pious soul, Paramhans Sant Hirdaram Sahib and the motivation and guidance of Rev. Siddh Bhauji, Shaheed Hemu Kalani Educational Society was formed on 25th November, 1996 and registered under M.P. Societies Registration Act, 1973. Venturing industriously in the field of education since 1996, the Society has been providing quality education right from pre-nursery to post graduation level through its six quality schools and two colleges.
