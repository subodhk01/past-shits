Control Statements

The statemens which control the way of execution of the programs are called control statements.
Control statements are divided into 3 categories :-

  1. Sequencial
  2. Branching
  3. Loops
1. Sequencial

Sequencial is the normal way in which our program flows. Our first program was a sequencial statement.

2. Branching

Branching is the widest part of control statements. It is further divided into 2 parts and they are further divided into 5 and 4 parts respectively.

3. Loops

Loops are type of statements in which our program repeats itself until the given condition is true.
Loops are of three types :-

  1. While Loop
  2. Do While Loop
  3. For Loop
